Our Aim

Disabilities

To help people with disabilities to become self reliant and to provide them with equal opportunities for employment or self employment.To establish and run special schools for persons with disabilities.To provide rehabilitation for leprosy cured persons (LCPS).

Health

To start, establish, run, take over or manage and maintain hospitals charitable dispensaries, maternity homes, child welfare centres, convalescent homes, sanatoriums, hostels and other similar institutions or centres after permission from concerned government authority.To health awareness programmes on HIV/AIDS. Pulse polio, RCH and any other programmes initiated by the government from time to time.

HUMAN RIGHTS

To campaign for human rights.To tackle critical human rights issues – such as gender based discrimination and violence, human trafficking, illegal immigration, slavery and bondage issues. The campaign would also cover conflicted and anti-insurgency areas and regions across the country.

Education

To establish and manage the vocational training institution in typing, shorthand, computer, fine art, crafts, music, painting, modeling, dancing, yoga, fashion designing, physical education, etc.To provide Scholarship, from books, educational aids charity to needy, deserving students/persons in performing and discharging their social and moral obligations and duties e. g. vocational training and academic education.To work for Formal and Non–formal education for the upliftment of the Society.

Environment

To create environmental awareness campaign and protection of natural resources.To develop strategies, technologies, methodologies and information for better environment management. To development biological interventions or pollution prevention.To develop microbial and other biological methods for treatment of solid and liquid waste and hazardous industrial wastes.To provide ambulances services for animals in distress and to provide shelter houses for looking after the animals.To provide rain harvesting in private and government sectors.

Community Development

To provide community development services to several poverty affected communities which includes healthcare, literacy classes, clean water & sanitation, vocational training for augmentation of earning, micro enterprise development and leadership training.Rural area Development

Disaster Relief

To provide relief & rehabilitation to people affected by any natural disaster like earthquake, draught, flood, famine, cyclone, landslide Tsunami, and other seismic ocean waves.To provide relief & rehabilitation towards the manmade disaster like nuclear, biological, chemical weapons or any other forms.To encourage the people for conservation of natural resources and to campaign for rain water harvesting and against other natural resources wastages.To provide relief measures in the affected areas during natural calamity.To provide emergency relief / assistance in any eventualities or disaster like flood, fire, accident, earthquake etc. to the community by adopting proper means and with all possible help & efforts.

Handicraft

To organize the artisan clusters into Self Help Groups (SHGs) Co-operatives with a view to enabling them to take up economic activity for sustainable development through a participatory role.T To empower artisans by making them active entrepreneurs cum primary stakeholders in the process of development and bringing them to a visible platform for easy access to domestic and overseas markets. To upgrade artisan’s skills through appropriate design and technology intervention so as to enable them to use quality raw material, tools and equipment to produce value added items.

About us

Our main moto is Child Development,Education to needy one,Disaster Relief for people who are affected by natural disaste,women empowerment programmes in rural/urban areas, To work against corruption in the community and hold seminars and workshops for awareness.
